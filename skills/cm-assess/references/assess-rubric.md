# Assessment Scoring Rubric

Each dimension is scored on a 3-level scale: **Weak** / **Adequate** / **Strong**.

## Scoring Standards

### 1. Content Specificity

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Entries describe only what was done; no actions or outcomes; generic descriptions |
| Adequate | Most entries cover what + how or what + result, but not all three dimensions |
| Strong | Every entry covers all three dimensions (what, how, result); specific enough to generate artifacts directly |

### 2. Quantification

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | No quantitative data captured; results described only qualitatively |
| Adequate | Some entries have numbers but context is missing (no baseline or scale) |
| Strong | Most entries have measurable outcomes with before/after or scale context |

### 3. Agency

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Personal contribution unclear; described at team level ("we built X") without individual role |
| Adequate | Some entries clarify individual role; others still at team level |
| Strong | Clear personal contribution in every entry; decision-making and ownership documented |

### 4. Narrative Coherence

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Sparse coverage; major career periods missing; no clear progression |
| Adequate | Most career periods covered but uneven depth; progression visible but not compelling |
| Strong | Comprehensive coverage; clear growth trajectory; gaps explained or addressed |

### 5. Readability (scenario-specific: resume only)

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Existing resume is a dense wall of text; no visual hierarchy; ATS-unfriendly |
| Adequate | Basic structure exists; key points findable with effort |
| Strong | 3-second scan reveals key strengths; clear headings; ATS-compatible |

### 6. Impact Breadth (scenario-specific: promotion only)

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | All entries are individual contributions; no evidence of broader impact |
| Adequate | Some entries show team or org-level impact; influence beyond individual role |
| Strong | Clear progression of scope; demonstrated influence at team/org level; mentoring/leadership evidence |

### 7. Goal Alignment (scenario-specific: performance review only)

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Entries don't connect to any stated goals or objectives |
| Adequate | Some entries align with goals; others are disconnected |
| Strong | Most entries clearly connect to goals; demonstrates consistent goal-oriented progress |

### 8. Narrative Arc (Enhanced: when 3+ entries exist)

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Entries read as an unconnected list of jobs; no progression, growth, or direction visible |
| Adequate | Some progression visible but not articulated; career story is implied but not compelling |
| Strong | Clear growth trajectory; each role builds on the previous; career direction is coherent and compelling |

### 9. Differentiation (Enhanced: when target_direction is set)

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Entries describe standard responsibilities anyone in the role would have; nothing unique |
| Adequate | Some unique contributions or contexts, but they're not highlighted or emphasized |
| Strong | Clear unique value proposition; specific capabilities, experiences, or perspectives that distinguish from typical candidates |

### 10. Evidence Density (Enhanced: always)

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Most claims are asserted without supporting evidence; "improved X" with no specifics |
| Adequate | Mix of supported and unsupported claims; key achievements have some evidence but not all |
| Strong | Nearly every claim is substantiated with specific evidence, metrics, or concrete examples from entries |

### 11. Target Alignment (Enhanced: when target_direction is set)

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Entries have no clear connection to the target direction; capabilities described don't support the stated goal |
| Adequate | Some entries are relevant to the target direction; key supporting evidence exists but gaps are visible |
| Strong | Entries clearly build a case for the target direction; capabilities, impact, and trajectory all support the stated goal |

### 12. Academic/Internship Depth (Enhanced: when career_stage = "graduate")

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | Academic and internship experiences described at surface level; no transferable skills or learning demonstrated |
| Adequate | Some academic/internship work shows transferable skills or practical application; depth varies |
| Strong | Academic and internship experiences are articulated with professional framing; transferable skills, learning velocity, and practical outcomes are clear |

### 13. Transferable Skills (Enhanced: when career_stage = "career-change")

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Weak | No evidence of skills that bridge old and new domains; career change rationale unsupported |
| Adequate | Some transferable skills identified but not explicitly mapped to the new domain; narrative is implied |
| Strong | Clear mapping of skills from previous domain to target domain; career change framed as a strength with specific bridges identified |

## Gap Classification

After scoring, classify each gap as:

- **[needs-capture]** — missing information requiring new entry capture. Specify:
  - What type of entry (project, signal, achievement)
  - What area (which company, which capability dimension)
  - Suggested capture mode (active description, session review, document import)

- **[needs-supplement]** — existing entry lacks depth in a specific dimension. Specify:
  - Which entry to supplement (by id)
  - Which dimension is weak (what, how, or result)
  - What specific detail is missing (metrics, approach, outcome, scope)
